What is Symbian OS

Symbian OS is microkernel based operating system explicitly targeted at several
geenralized platforms.

It is flexible enough to meet the industry's requirements for developing a variety
of advanced mobile devices and phones, while allowing manufacturers the oportunity
to differentiate their products.

It was also decided that Symbian would actively adopt current state-of-teh-art key
technologies as they become available. This decision reinforced the design choices
of object oriented and client-server architecture.
