(o) real-time - special purpose computer system where rigid time requirements have been
    placed on the processor or input/output operations. These time limits are well defined,
    and a failure occurs when these limits are not met. The real-time system split into:
    - hard-realtime systems - guarantee that time constraints are met.
    - soft-realtime systems - place a priority on time-critical processes.
